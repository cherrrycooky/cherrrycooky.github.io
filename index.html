Research

I work on statistical methods for measuring dependence structures in complex data, especially when variables have mixed types or when conditional relationships are involved. A second line of my work concerns statistical disclosure risk and privacy assessment for synthetic data.

Dependence measures for mixed-type data

Nonparametric association measures, categorical responses, nearest-neighbor methods, conditional dependence, and interpretable measures of signal strength.

Synthetic data privacy

Attribution disclosure risk, utility-risk tradeoffs, and general frameworks for privacy risk assessment in synthetic data releases.
